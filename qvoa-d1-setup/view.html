Calculation Notes
  • Amount of Resting Cells = (Number of Wells + 1) x (Resting Cell Dilution)
  • Volume Resting Cells = (Amount Resting Cells) / (Cell Stock Concentration)
  • Amount of Irradiated Cells = (Number of Wells + 1) x (5-fold Excess of Resting Cell Dilution)
  • Volume Irradiated Cells = (Amount Irradiated Cells) / (Irradiated Cell Stock Concentration)
  • Volume cIMDM = (Number of Wells + 1 x Volume per Well) - (Sum of all cell & reagent volumes)
